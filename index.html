ABOUT US

We Are Driving Innovation, Collaboration, and Growth Across Africa

Orbit Africa is a platform designed to foster collaboration among tech hubs, entrepreneurs, and investors across the African continent. By providing access to resources, tools, and cross-border opportunities, we empower the tech ecosystem to innovate and tackle challenges in healthcare, agriculture, and beyond.

Mission
To empower Africa's tech ecosystem by building a unified network of innovation hubs that fosters collaboration, resource sharing, and skill development. Through a central digital platform, cross-border initiatives, and inclusive programs, we aim to connect communities, accelerate tech-driven solutions, and drive sustainable development across the continent.
Vision
To create a truly united African tech community that transforms the continent into a global leader in innovation. By seamlessly connecting talent, resources, and ideas across borders, we envision an ecosystem where every hub contributes to Africa’s growth, enabling shared prosperity, inclusive opportunities, and groundbreaking solutions to the continent’s most pressing challenges.
SERVICES

What We Offer

We bridge the gap within Africa’s tech communities by creating a dynamic network that fosters collaboration, innovation, and seamless resource sharing.

Digital Platform

OrbitA’s digital platform provides a central space for tech hubs to connect, share knowledge, and collaborate on impactful projects.

OrbitA Passport Membership

A membership program offering seamless access to participating hubs, mentorship programs, and funding opportunities across Africa.

Cross-Border Collaboration

Support for large-scale, multi-country projects tackling critical challenges like healthcare, education, and agriculture.
